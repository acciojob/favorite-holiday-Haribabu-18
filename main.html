Goa


Goa is a popular holiday destination in India known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich culture, and delicious seafood. It offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for its nightlife and water sports
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the Queen of Beaches
  3. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A historic church and UNESCO site
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India
  5. Fort Aguada – A historic Portuguese fort with sea views
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Bebinca – A traditional Goan dessert
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Chicken Cafreal
